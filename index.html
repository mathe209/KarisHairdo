About Me

I am a self-taught hairstylist with a passion for braiding. I have been doing hair for over 5 years and I love what I do. I am based in Cape Town, South Africa and I am available for bookings.

I specialize in knotless braids, jumbo braids, and other styles. I use high-quality hair products to ensure that your hair looks great and stays healthy.

I am available for bookings on weekends and public holidays. Please contact me for more information.
